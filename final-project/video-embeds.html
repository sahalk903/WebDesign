    How to get a video to play on your website?

    To have vidoes play on your website, you need to know how to embed videos. This process is created by the programmer using the video in their code.

    How to embed videos from Youtube?

    1.Access the Youtube video you wish to use and click the "share" button.

    2. Then, click the "embed" button.

    3. Look at the settings and make sure they are correct for what you want. Then copy the iframe tag.

    4. Lastly, paste the copied iframe tag into your code. Then, the video should appear on your website.

    How to embed videos from a computer file?

    1. Access your video file on your device.

    2. Create a "video" folder within the folder for website you are creating. Then place the actual video in the folder.

    3. Create a source tag that is in a video tag and type in the video's file name.

    4. Check the settings such as preload, muted, autoplay, and width of the video are to your liking.
